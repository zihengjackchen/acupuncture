How it works

How to start

From the first conversation to follow-up planning, the process stays clear and easy to understand.

1. Start with a brief consult

We review your main concern, answer questions, and confirm whether mobile care is the right fit.

2. Receive care at home

Your visit includes a full intake, TCM assessment, and treatment shaped to your symptoms and stage of healing.

3. Build momentum with follow-up

We set a starting rhythm, track your response, and adjust as your symptoms, cycle, or stress load changes.
