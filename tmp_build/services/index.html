How the treatment process works

Our process is straightforward and clearly paced so you know what happens before treatment starts and how follow-up care is planned.

1. Brief virtual consultation before booking

Start with a brief virtual consultation to review your main concern, discuss your goals, and confirm that treatment is a good fit. This consultation is $20 and is required before further booking.

2. Mobile visit and first treatment

Your acupuncturist comes to you for a full intake, pulse and tongue assessment, and your first treatment. The mobile service base commute rate is $29.99 per commute.

3. Follow-up plan and frequency

Follow-up frequency depends on your condition and how long it has been going on. For severe pain, we often recommend twice-weekly visits. For many other concerns, weekly treatment for four to six weeks is a reasonable starting point before judging progress.

4. Fees, discounts, and support

Rates are kept fair and plans are personalized. We offer package options and senior discounts. Call 630-408-7875 for current package and discount details.

Safety and suitability

Licensed acupuncturists are trained in safe needling techniques. Minor bleeding, bruising, or dizziness can happen in some patients. Extra caution is used for people taking blood thinners. Electro-acupuncture may not be appropriate for people with pacemakers, and non-electric treatment is available.
