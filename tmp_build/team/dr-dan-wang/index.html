Dr. Dan Wang

Licensed Acupuncturist & Herbalist

Originally from Northeast China, Dan brings a rich cultural and clinical background rooted in Traditional Chinese Medicine. She earned her degree from the Liaoning University of Traditional Chinese Medicine in the 1990s and has been practicing in the western suburbs of Chicago for the past four years.

Dan is deeply passionate about Chinese medical philosophy and integrates practices such as Taiji, Yijing, and Feng Shui into her holistic approach. She believes true healing comes from achieving balance, both mentally and physically, and she works closely with each patient to support that harmony.

She has a special interest in treating emotional health concerns such as stress and anxiety, as well as women’s health issues including menstrual irregularities, fertility challenges, postpartum recovery, sleep disorders, and menopause symptoms.

Dan also supports patients throughout natural conception, IUI, and IVF journeys in coordination with their medical care team. She is known for her compassionate presence and her ability to truly listen to each patient’s unique story.

Outside the clinic, Dan enjoys reading, listening to music, and spending time with her family and friends.
