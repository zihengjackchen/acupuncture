# Mobile acupuncture care in Chicagoland.

Wang's Harmony Acupuncture LLC brings personalized acupuncture care to homes across the Chicagoland area. Treatment is rooted in Traditional Chinese Medicine and shaped around your symptoms, health history, and goals.

We commonly support pain, injury recovery, fertility and IVF care, postpartum recovery, stress, sleep concerns, digestion issues, sinus symptoms, and menopause care. Every treatment plan starts with a conversation so you know what to expect and what the next steps are.
