Originally from Northeast China, Dan brings a deep cultural and clinical background in Traditional Chinese Medicine. She earned her degree from the Liaoning University of Traditional Chinese Medicine in the 1990s and has practiced in the western suburbs of Chicago for the past four years.

Dan is deeply interested in Chinese medical philosophy and believes healing is strongest when care is calm, personalized, and grounded in balance. She works closely with each patient to understand their symptoms, explain the treatment plan clearly, and support steady progress over time.

She has a special interest in stress, anxiety, sleep concerns, and women's health, including menstrual irregularities, fertility challenges, postpartum recovery, and menopause support.

Dan also supports patients through natural conception, IUI, and IVF in coordination with their medical care team. She is known for her compassionate presence and for listening closely to each patient's story.

Outside the clinic, Dan enjoys reading, listening to music, and spending time with her family and friends.
