Some of the most common reasons patients come to us include:

- [Pain and injury recovery](/services/pain-and-injury-management/), including neck pain, back pain, headaches, and mobility concerns
- [Women's health and fertility support](/services/womens-health-and-fertility/), including cycle health, IVF support, postpartum recovery, and menopause
- [Stress, sleep, and wellness support](/services/stress-sleep-and-wellness/), including insomnia, anxiety, fatigue, and burnout
- [Digestive and metabolic support](/services/digestive-and-metabolic-health/), including IBS, bloating, reflux, and appetite changes
- [Respiratory, immune, and skin support](/services/respiratory-immune-and-skin/), including sinus symptoms, allergies, cough, and repeat flare-ups

Acupuncture can be a useful part of a broader care plan, but it does not replace needed medical evaluation. For example, after an injury it is smart to see a medical doctor first to rule out fractures or structural damage before starting treatment.

If you are not sure which service area matches your symptoms, review [Services and Fees](/services/) or [contact the clinic](/contact/) and we can help you choose a starting point.
