The most common concerns patients visit us for include pain, anxiety, sinus congestion, insomnia, energy improvement, ear problems, digestion issues, fertility, IVF support, postpartum care, and menopause support.

Acupuncture can help reduce pain after injury, but it is wise to see a medical doctor first to rule out fractures and structural damage before beginning acupuncture treatment.
