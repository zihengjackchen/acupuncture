The most common concerns patients visit us for include:

- Pain
- Anxiety
- Sinus congestion
- Insomnia
- Energy improvement
- Ear problems
- Digestion issues
- Fertility
- IVF support
- Postpartum care
- Menopause support

Acupuncture can help reduce pain after injury, but it is wise to see a medical doctor first to rule out fractures and structural damage before beginning acupuncture treatment.
