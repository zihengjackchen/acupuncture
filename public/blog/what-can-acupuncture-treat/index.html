Questions

What Can Acupuncture Treat?

See the main concerns Wang's Harmony Acupuncture commonly supports, from pain and stress to fertility, digestion, sleep, and sinus symptoms.

Some of the most common reasons patients come to us include:

  • Pain and injury recovery, including neck pain, back pain, headaches, and mobility concerns
  • Women’s health and fertility support, including cycle health, IVF support, postpartum recovery, and menopause
  • Stress, sleep, and wellness support, including insomnia, anxiety, fatigue, and burnout
  • Digestive and metabolic support, including IBS, bloating, reflux, and appetite changes
  • Respiratory, immune, and skin support, including sinus symptoms, allergies, cough, and repeat flare-ups

Acupuncture can be a useful part of a broader care plan, but it does not replace needed medical evaluation. For example, after an injury it is smart to see a medical doctor first to rule out fractures or structural damage before starting treatment.

If you are not sure which service area matches your symptoms, review Services and Fees or contact the clinic and we can help you choose a starting point.
