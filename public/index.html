How it works

How care flows from first contact to follow-up

From the first conversation to follow-up planning, the process stays clear and easy to understand.

1. Start with a brief consult

We review your main concern, goals, and whether mobile acupuncture is the right fit before moving into treatment planning.

2. Receive care at home

Your appointment includes a full intake, TCM assessment, and a treatment experience tailored to your symptoms and stage of healing.

3. Build momentum with follow-up

We recommend an initial treatment rhythm, check how you're responding, and adjust as your symptoms, cycle, or stress load changes.
