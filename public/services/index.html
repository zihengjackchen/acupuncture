How care works

The process is simple: start with a brief consult, schedule care, and follow a treatment plan that matches your symptoms.

1. Brief virtual consultation before booking

Start with a $20 virtual consultation to review your concern, goals, and whether treatment is a good fit.

2. Mobile visit and first treatment

Your acupuncturist comes to you for a full intake, pulse and tongue assessment, and your first treatment.

3. Follow-up plan and frequency

Follow-up depends on your condition. Severe pain may need twice-weekly care, while many other concerns start with weekly visits for four to six weeks.

4. Fees, discounts, and support

Rates stay clear, plans stay personalized, and package options or senior discounts are available by phone.

Safety and suitability

Licensed acupuncturists are trained in safe needling. Minor bruising, light bleeding, or dizziness can happen. Extra caution is used with blood thinners, and non-electric treatment is available if electro-acupuncture is not appropriate.
